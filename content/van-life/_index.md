We didn't set out to live in a van. We set out to stop waiting.

Waiting for the right time. The right savings number. The right moment when everything lined up and life finally said *go.* The van was just what made it possible to stop negotiating with all of that.

Seven years later, we've lived in more square footage of open sky than most people see in a lifetime. We've woken up in national forests, slept through desert storms, and figured out — through a lot of trial and a lot of error — what it actually takes to make a small space feel like home.

This section is where we write about what that life is really like. Not the highlight reel. Not the gear list. The texture of it — the freedom, the friction, and everything in between.
