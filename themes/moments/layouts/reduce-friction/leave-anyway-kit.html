Why This Exists

You don't have an information problem.

You know it's possible. You've read the articles. You follow the accounts. You've done the math in your head more times than you'd like to admit.

What you have is a permission problem.

And underneath that: money that doesn't feel real enough, a job you don't know how to leave, people in your life who don't understand, and a version of yourself that keeps moving the finish line.

This kit is for that.

What It Is

Not a course. Not a checklist. A workspace.

The Leave Anyway Kit is a 10-section Notion workspace — part guided journal, part honest framework, part practical planning tool.

Each section pairs a real story from the road with reflection prompts, decision tools, and the kind of specific, unglamorous guidance you don't find in travel content. The kind that only comes from having actually done it.

It doesn't tell you to leave. It helps you figure out whether you're ready — and if you are, what's actually in the way.

Your workspace. Your pace. No deadline.

Money

Whether you're leaving it, remote-ing it, or figuring that part out — this section covers the honest options, the conversations worth having, and what we've seen work.

Stuff

The great purge. What to keep, what to let go, and how to stop using "I don't know what to do with my stuff" as a reason to stay.

Mail. Insurance. Banking that works across borders. The phone plan. The things nobody puts in the inspirational content because they're not romantic — but that will absolutely derail you if you don't handle them before you leave.

Coverage that doesn't require a permanent address. What to do before you go. What we've actually used out there.

09

Relationships

What it's really like to travel as a couple. What it's like to leave people behind. What the road does to the people you love — the honest version, not the highlight reel.

10

The first 30 days

The part after you leave. What actually happens, what usually surprises people, and how to give yourself the best chance of making it past the moment when you wonder what you were thinking.

FAQ

Do I need to know Notion already?

No. The template is set up and ready to use. If you've never opened Notion before, there's a short orientation at the top of the workspace.

Is this for van life specifically?

The examples draw on our van life experience, but the framework applies to any long-term travel — sabbatical, slow travel, extended trip, or full location independence.

What if I've already started planning?

Jump to whichever sections are still live for you. You don't have to work through it in order.

Because this is a digital product delivered instantly, we don't offer refunds. If something isn't working with your access, reach out and we'll sort it.
